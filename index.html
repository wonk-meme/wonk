WONK BURN BOT

The WONK Burn Bot is an innovative feature designed to enhance the value and scarcity of WONK tokens. This automated system actively participates in the market, strategically acquiring and burning WONK tokens to reduce the total supply over time.

By reducing the circulating supply, the Burn Bot creates a deflationary pressure, which can positively impact the token's price and benefit all WONK holders. This mechanism is transparent and verifiable on the blockchain, ensuring trust and accountability.

The bot's operations are governed by smart contracts, ensuring fairness and preventing manipulation. It's a testament to WONK's commitment to building a sustainable and valuable ecosystem for its community.
